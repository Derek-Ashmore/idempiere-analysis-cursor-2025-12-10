## Decision Matrix

### Choose **Odoo** if:
- ✅ You need open-source flexibility
- ✅ Customization is important
- ✅ Budget is a primary concern
- ✅ You can accept medium migration complexity

### Choose **ERPNext** if:
- ✅ You prefer modern, cloud-native architecture
- ✅ You're a small-to-medium enterprise
- ✅ You want lower cost than commercial solutions
- ✅ You can accept medium migration complexity

### Choose **NetSuite** if:
- ✅ You need enterprise-grade support
- ✅ You want comprehensive features out-of-box
- ✅ Budget allows for commercial solution
- ✅ You prefer vendor-managed infrastructure

### Choose **Dynamics 365 BC** if:
- ✅ You're already in Microsoft ecosystem
- ✅ Office 365/Teams integration is valuable
- ✅ You want Power Platform integration
- ✅ You prefer Microsoft's support model

### Choose **SAP ByDesign** if:
- ✅ You need SAP's enterprise capabilities
- ✅ You have complex manufacturing needs
- ✅ You're mid-market but need enterprise features
- ✅ Budget allows for SAP investment

### Choose **Oracle ERP Cloud** if:
- ✅ You're a large enterprise
- ✅ You need Oracle ecosystem integration
- ✅ You have complex global requirements
- ✅ Budget is not a primary constraint

### Choose **SAP S/4HANA** if:
- ✅ You're a very large, complex enterprise
- ✅ You have existing SAP investment
- ✅ You need maximum functionality
- ✅ Budget and timeline are flexible

## Critical Migration Considerations

### Must Assess Before Selection:

1. **Data Volume**
   - < 1M records: Low complexity
   - 1M-10M records: Medium complexity
   - > 10M records: High complexity

2. **Customization Level**
   - Standard config only: Low effort
   - Some custom code: Medium effort
   - Extensive customization: High effort

3. **Integration Requirements**
   - Standalone: Low complexity
   - Few integrations: Medium complexity
   - Many integrations: High complexity

4. **Timeline Constraints**
   - < 12 months: Consider simpler solutions
   - 12-18 months: Most solutions viable
   - > 18 months: All solutions viable
